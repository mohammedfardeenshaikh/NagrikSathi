# Priority Classification Skill

## Purpose

Determine the urgency of every complaint.

---

## Priority Levels

### Critical

Immediate danger to public safety.

Examples

- Bridge collapse
- Major water contamination
- Hospital emergency

---

### High

Serious infrastructure issue affecting many citizens.

Examples

- Large potholes
- Water shortage
- Broken traffic signal

---

### Medium

Important issue that should be addressed soon.

Examples

- Garbage collection delay
- Street light not working

---

### Low

Minor inconvenience.

Examples

- Park maintenance
- Painting public walls

---

## Output

Priority

Reason

Confidence Score
